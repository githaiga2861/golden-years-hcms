# Phased Roadmap

## Phase 1 — this repo (essentials, running smoothly)
Registration · scheduling (incl. weekly recurring + open shifts) · availability · care plans/ADLs ·
Care App with GPS clock in/out and offline sync · visit notes · alerts (location mismatch, late/missed
clock-in, missing clock-out) · verified-hours review · invoice generation · payroll CSV · roles · settings ·
physician orders & medication documents.

## Phase 2 — refine with the team
- QuickBooks Online sync (invoices → A/R, hours → payroll) — OAuth app under the agency's Intuit account
- In-app messaging (tables and RLS already in place)
- Push notifications (new shift assigned, shift reminders)
- Mileage tracking between clients (GPS trips → payroll line items)
- Client/family e-signature at end of visit
- Live-in caregiver placements (multi-day, sleep-time carve-outs, overtime toggle)

## Phase 3 — grow
- Family portal (schedules, visit history, invoices)
- Online payment collection (card/ACH) on invoices
- Physician/care-team communication hub
- Deeper reporting & analytics
